ABOUT US

Guiding students to the right path

Pathlights is a student-focused career guidance platform designed to bridge the gap between education and industry expectations. We empower school students to discover suitable career paths and support college students in building the skills and confidence required for successful placements. Through practical guidance, real-world insights, and structured mentorship, Pathlights aims to prepare students for a future filled with opportunities.

💡

Vision

To become a trusted guide that empowers students to choose the right career path with clarity and confidence.

🎯

Mission

  • Prepare college students for placements through structured practice and feedback
  • Help students after 12th understand career options and make informed decisions
  • Bridge the gap between education and industry expectations

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pathlights?
Pathlights is a career guidance initiative helping students prepare for placements and choose the right career path.
Who can benefit from Pathlights services?
Pre-final & final year college students and students after Hifher Secondary seeking structured guidance.
Does Pathlights guarantee job placements?
We enhance readiness and confidence through structured preparation, though placements are not guaranteed.
What does a mock interview include?
Real-world interview simulation, communication analysis, and detailed performance feedback.
How are sessions conducted?
Sessions are conducted one-to-one or in small focused groups, online or offline.
